Project Overview

This project involved a comprehensive technical analysis of battery performance and power consumption across a range of commercially available smartwatches. The goal was to establish a set of standardized efficiency metrics for wearable devices. Our findings detail the trade-offs between display refresh rates, sensor activity (e.g., heart rate monitoring, GPS), and overall device longevity. The primary deliverable was a detailed report proposing design recommendations to extend battery life by up to 35% without significant component changes. This documentation serves as a guide for product managers and engineering teams to prioritize low-power solutions in future development cycles.
